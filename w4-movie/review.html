REVIEW

★★★★★

Devin Rivera

February 2, 2023

A Bronx Tale is a story based in the inner boroughs of the Bronx in New York. It follows a kid named Calogero and his life living in the bronx. He grows up admiring the local mob boss named Sonny; his father, Lorenzo sees this and wants his son to have nothing to do with the mob. I love this movie because while it does give a good sense of action and suspense, throughout the movie a lot of life long lessons are taught. One my favorite quotes are mentioned in the movie and it goes as follows: "The saddest thing in life is wasted talent"
